What Are Fibroids?

Fibroids are common non-cancerous growths that develop in or around the uterus and may cause symptoms that affect daily life.

Common Symptoms

Heavy menstrual bleeding
Pelvic pain or pressure
Bloating
Frequent urination
Lower back pain
Fatigue

Treatment Options

Watchful Waiting
Medication
Myomectomy
Hysterectomy
UFE

Why Choose UFE?

  • No large surgical incision
  • Uterus preserved
  • Short hospital stay
  • Faster recovery
  • Minimal scarring
  • Relief from heavy bleeding and pressure symptoms
